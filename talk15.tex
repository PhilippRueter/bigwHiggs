\begin{definition}
    A parabolic bundle $ E \rightarrow \bar{X} $ over compact Riemann surface $ \bar{X}$ 
    is a bundle together with a subset $ D \subset \bar{X}$, 
    such that for each $p \in D$, we have a full flag

        E|_p > E|_{1,p} > \dots > E|_{r , p } > \{ 0 \} \\

    \end{align}
\end{definition}
